About


How many times have you heard your friend post a picture to Instagram and immediately say, "How many likes do you think it will get? I bet at least 20"? Or that one time when you thought you posted the perfect photo, only to get zero likes... Do you have the ability to determine if a photo is epic or just another selfie? When your friends post a photo on Instagram, users will be able to guess how (un)popular that photo will be. Bet virtual currency on how many likes you think your friends' photos will get. The closer you get, the more you win! Socialbookie provides the platform for the guessing game we all have been playing verbally since the advent of the "Like". The Instagram social experience is now more fun and lively. Currently for Android and launching on iOS soon.
