Partners: Benefits to retailers


More data

Roughly 25% of retail customers pay in cash. As such, retailers have no customer data for 1/4 of their clientele. By creating a footprint for this customer base through Coin Out, retailers can obtain an entirely new, valuable pool of data

More traffic

By establishing a new customer footprint, Coin Out will create an additional channel of communication for retailers where they can offer exclusive deals, coupons, etc outside of their own App

More profit

Coin Out will cut down the operating cost for retailers by simply reducing the coins needed to stock the registers
